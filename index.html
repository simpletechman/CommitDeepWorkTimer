Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Commit is intentionally simple. It does one thing well: shows you whether you kept your word today.

⏱

Menu Bar Timer

Always-visible countdown in your menu bar shows time remaining, surplus, or deficit at a glance. Never lose track of where you stand.

🎯

Daily Commitments

Set a daily goal from 30 minutes to 8 hours. Start and stop sessions throughout the day — they all accumulate toward your total.

📉

Deficit Rollover Pro

Fell short yesterday? Today's goal automatically increases by the deficit (capped at 2× your goal) so you stay genuinely accountable.

📅

Session History

Review the last 7 days free, or your full history with Pro. Color-coded daily tiles — green for met, red for missed — show the truth at a glance.

📊

Weekly Report Pro

A simple bar chart of hours vs. goal for each day of the week. Spot your patterns and adjust your commitments accordingly.

🗓

Custom Active Days Pro

Choose any combination of days — not just Mon–Fri. Weekend warrior or unconventional schedule? Commit adapts to you.

How It Works

Up and running in 60 seconds

No account. No setup. Just open the app and start working.

1

Set your daily commitment

Choose how many hours of deep work you want to do today. You can change this any time.

2

Start a session when you sit down to work

One tap to start, one tap to stop. Every session counts toward your daily total — no minimum duration.

3

Watch the menu bar

Commit shows your running deficit or surplus throughout the day. Green means you're ahead. Red means you need to get back to work.

4

End the day with the truth

Did you keep your commitment? The history view doesn't sugarcoat it. That's the point.
