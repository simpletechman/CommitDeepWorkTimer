Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

The short version: Commit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

1. Overview

Commit ("the App") is a macOS application developed by Sahil Pabby ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our core principle is simple: your data is yours and stays on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your local device.

The App stores the following data locally on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Your daily commitment goal (hours per day)
  • Work session start and end times
  • Which days of the week you have marked as active
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored in your device's app container and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports (beyond what Apple may collect through the App Store)
  • Any data about what you work on or what apps you use

4. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Commit offers optional Pro subscriptions through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit). All billing, payment processing, and subscription management is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment information, and Apple's privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

To manage or cancel your subscription, visit your Apple ID settings on your Mac or iPhone.

5. Apple's Data Practices

As the App is distributed through the Mac App Store, Apple may collect certain diagnostic and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. This data is collected by Apple, not by us. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Third-Party Services

Commit does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud providers. The App has zero external dependencies.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your Mac's own security mechanisms including FileVault encryption (if enabled) and macOS's app sandboxing. We have no access to your data and cannot retrieve it under any circumstances.

8. Children's Privacy

Commit is not directed at children under 13 years of age. Because we collect no personal data from any users, no special provisions are needed; however, we encourage parents to monitor their children's use of all software.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Any future changes will never contradict our core principle of not collecting your personal data without your explicit consent.
